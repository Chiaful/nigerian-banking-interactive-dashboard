Transparency Statement
This dashboard is a strategic scenario modelling tool. Projections are computed from verified baseline figures using compound growth rate formulae — consistent with methodology used by the IMF, World Bank, McKinsey, and PwC in sector planning tools. All baseline figures are drawn from primary, publicly verifiable Nigerian and international data sources listed below.
Projection Methodology
Growth Variables
Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) applied to 2025 baselines over 10 years. Formula: V₂₀₃₅ = V₂₀₂₅ × (1 + r)¹⁰ where r is the slider-adjusted annual rate.
Inclusion Variables
Linear interpolation between 2025 baseline and an upper-bound ceiling (e.g. 95% max inclusion), modulated by the slider rate. Ceilings reflect structural limits observed in comparable emerging markets.
